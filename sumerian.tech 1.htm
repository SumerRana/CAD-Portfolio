ABOUT ME

I am a third-year mechanical and automation engineering student pursuing my degree at G. B. Pant Govt. Engineering college, Okhla. My knowledge in mechanical as well as automation field gives me a wider perspective and makes me ready for ever-changing industries of CAD, CAM and CAE, and Engineering in general. Being at a reputed government college has opened doors of exposure and opportunities making me ahead of my competition already.

My primary skills include modelling and analysis in SolidWorks in which I have won an inter-college competition, that too during the period of my early learning. The fact that we can design, assemble, analyse,disassemble, and repeat without creating the component or system in physical world really excites me.

I am passionate about designing and building machine parts and systems, and look to further my abilities at CAD and CAE, and engineering in general.

My inner drive for improvements and optimal performance is complemented by a sense of humility to stay focused on my goals. I live by the motto “learning should never stop” and am excited about what next there is to experience and learn. Being a gifted learner I have a tendancy to learn quickly.
